last Monday

Happy to launch the fully digital CDC Vouchers Scheme at Jurong Spring CC today. Every Singaporean household will receive $100 of CDC vouchers, to use for daily expenses like food, goods and services. Delighted that over 200,000 households have already claimed their vouchers. To find out more about the vouchers, please go to https://vouchers.cdc.gov.sg/residents/info.

This scheme was part of the Household Support Package announced in the Budget in February. It will also help... heartland shops and hawkers, who have been hit hard during this pandemic. You can collect and use the vouchers using your phone. I did it myself, and found it quick and convenient!

The vouchers are the Government’s way of thanking all Singaporeans for your solidarity and support during this difficult period. We are not out of the woods yet, but we are adapting well to living with the virus. – LHL
